ShareNow Privacy Policy

Your files stay yours.

ShareNow is built to transfer files directly between devices. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app can access, how that information is used, and when limited technical data may be processed to improve app stability and performance.

Effective date: April 25, 2026

1. Information ShareNow may access

  • Files and file metadata: When you choose files to send, ShareNow accesses selected file names, file size, and file content only to complete the transfer.
  • Camera access: Camera permission is used only to scan QR codes for device connection.
  • Wi-Fi and local network information: ShareNow uses Wi-Fi, hotspot, IP address, and port information to discover and connect nearby devices for peer-to-peer transfers.
  • Device name: Your device name may be shown to nearby devices during the connection process so that users can identify the correct sender or receiver.
  • Storage access: ShareNow uses storage access to read selected files and save received files on your device, including inside the Downloads/ShareNow folder on supported Android devices.
  • App diagnostics and analytics: ShareNow's Android build may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage patterns and diagnose crashes or stability issues.

2. How we use this information

  • To establish direct device-to-device connections.
  • To transfer files selected by the user.
  • To save received files on the receiving device.
  • To show transfer progress, transfer history, and connection status.
  • To identify and fix crashes, bugs, and reliability issues.
  • To improve app performance and user experience over time.

3. How file transfers work

ShareNow is designed for local file sharing between devices. File content is transferred directly between participating devices over local network or hotspot-based connection methods. ShareNow does not sell your files and is not intended to upload your personal files to a remote server as part of the core transfer experience.

4. Third-party services

ShareNow may use Google Firebase services in supported builds, including:

  • Firebase Analytics for aggregated app usage insights.
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and technical diagnostics.

These services may process technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and usage events according to Google's own privacy practices.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only in these limited cases:

  • With the other device you intentionally connect to for file transfer.
  • With service providers such as Firebase for analytics and crash reporting.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect users and the app.

6. Data retention

  • Files you receive are stored on your device until you delete them.
  • Transfer history shown inside the app may reflect files saved on your device.
  • Diagnostic or analytics information handled by third-party services may be retained under those providers' policies.

7. Permissions used by ShareNow

  • Camera: To scan QR codes.
  • Photos, media, and files / storage: To read files you choose and save received files.
  • Wi-Fi and nearby network access: To create and manage local file-sharing connections.
  • Notifications: To show transfer progress and completion status.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect connection data, transfer sessions, and stored files. However, no software or network method can guarantee absolute security. You should only connect to devices you trust and review file selections before sending.

9. Children's privacy

ShareNow is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app's core file-sharing features.

10. Your choices

  • You can deny optional permissions, though some features may stop working.
  • You can delete received files from your device at any time.
  • You can stop using the app whenever you choose.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app changes, legal requirements, or improved explanations. The updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised effective date.
